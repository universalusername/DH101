# Week 2 Reflection

Reflect 2: Can AI help us define what it means to be human, or does it reduce us to patterns?

AI sheds light on the nature of humanity by recognizing patterns in what we want to see and hear through the prompts and information we provide. In this way, we become a predictable variable in its never-ending game of “what’s next?” However, because AI cannot experience independently, assign meaning to what it generates (text, images, or other outputs) or create truly original content, it is incapable of defining the human experience in a new way. This is especially true given that every human changes every day, constantly redefining their existence through their own actions, reactions to others, and interactions with the world around them. In this sense, AI reduces us to patterns: a reflection of insights that cannot keep pace with the speed of human discovery, incapable of representing what we have not yet concretely expressed.
